How the Interactive Book + Website Work Together

1. Colour & Play

Each page in the book has a big, friendly dinosaur scene and a small QR code. Kids colour the page, complete the mini mission (find eggs, finish a maze, trace the name) and feel like real dino explorers.

2. Scan the QR Code

A grown-up scans the QR code with a phone or tablet. The code takes them to the matching section on this website ‚Äì for example the T. rex ice cream stand page or the Brachiosaurus treehouse page.

3. Unlock Bonus Content

On the dino's page they can see simple facts, a quick challenge or movement game, plus optional links to recommended dinosaur toys, kits or resources (using affiliate links, clearly labelled).

4. Keep Learning Off the Page

Kids can print extra pages, do dino yoga moves, or recreate scenes using craft ideas ‚Äì turning one colouring book into a whole mini dino-learning hub.

ü¶ñ T. rex Ice Cream Stand

In the book: T. rex runs a silly ice cream stand, using tiny arms to serve giant cones.

Quick Facts

  • Name: Tyrannosaurus rex (“tyrant lizard king”)
  • Diet: Carnivore (meat-eater)
  • Fun idea: Count how many scoops your T. rex has and colour each scoop a different flavour.

Try This Mini Challenge

“T-Rex Treat Tally” ‚Äì after colouring, ask your child:

  • How many cones can you see?
  • How many tiny arms does T. rex have?
  • Can you find something that didn't exist in dinosaur times? (Hint: ice cream!)
